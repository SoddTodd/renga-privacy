Privacy Policy for Renga

Last Updated: February 2026

Renga is a poetry and journaling app designed to help users write daily haiku and participate in collaborative renga poems. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Renga handles your data.

Information We Collect

Renga does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. All haiku entries and renga data are stored locally on your device using on-device storage. If you choose to use iCloud or device backups, your data may be backed up according to Apple’s iCloud and device backup policies.

Notifications

Renga may request permission to send you daily notifications to remind you to write a haiku. These notifications are generated locally on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

PhotoShrink uses Google Photos APIs to upload photos selected by the user.

In-App Purchases

Renga may offer optional in-app purchases to support development. All payments are handled securely by Apple. Renga does not receive or store your payment information.

Data Sharing

Renga does not share your data with any third parties.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete your data at any time by deleting the app.

Children’s Privacy

Renga does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer via the App Store listing.
